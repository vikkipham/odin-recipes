Old-Fashioned Carrot Cake

Description

This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread — white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¾ cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Step 1: Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Step 2: Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Step 3: Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Step 4: Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  5. Step 5:Serve hot
